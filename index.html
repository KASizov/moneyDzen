Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

MoneyDzen ("we", "the app") is a personal and family finance management application. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app's functionality:

  • Email address — used solely for account registration and authentication via Supabase.
  • Financial data — transactions, accounts, budgets, and savings goals that you enter manually. This data is stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, synchronized with Supabase servers.
  • Receipt photos — taken or selected by you for expense transactions. Stored locally and optionally uploaded to Supabase Storage. Used for OCR (text recognition) to auto-fill the amount field. OCR processing is done entirely on-device (offline) via Google ML Kit.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not track your behavior or show advertising.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not access your contacts, microphone, or camera without your explicit action.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase Backend & Auth
    Stores your account (email, hashed password) and synchronized financial data on secure servers. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • Google Sign-In Optional
    If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive only your email address and Google account ID. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google ML Kit On-device OCR
    Used to recognize text in receipt photos. All processing happens on your device — no data is sent to Google servers.

5. Local Data Storage

Financial data is stored locally in an SQLite database on your device. This data is accessible only to the MoneyDzen app. When you sign out, all local data is automatically deleted from your device.

6. Push Notifications

The app can send local push notifications to remind you of upcoming recurring payments (e.g., subscriptions). These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any external servers. You can disable them at any time in your device settings.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time via Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. Upon deletion:

  • All local data is removed from your device immediately.
  • Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted from Supabase servers.
  • This action is irreversible.

8. Data Security

  • All data transmitted to Supabase is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are never stored — Supabase uses industry-standard hashed authentication.
  • Access to your data in Supabase is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — no other user can access your data.

9. Children's Privacy

MoneyDzen is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
